I'm a product-oriented Engineering Manager with over a decade of experience building and scaling high-impact digital platforms. At Gartner Digital Markets, I lead the Reviews organization, the engine behind millions of user reviews across Capterra, GetApp, and Software Advice.

My work sits at the intersection of technical architecture, product strategy, and people leadership. I care deeply about developer experience, engineering culture, and the balance between strong architecture and pragmatic delivery.

Colleagues describe me as a connector: someone who unites teams after change, keeps communication open, and helps people grow through coaching and direct feedback. I've built teams from scratch, unified siloed functions, and consistently earned Exceeds Expectations performance reviews.

Away from work, I'm a father of three young boys (6, 4, and 5 months). They've taught me more about empathy, patience, and prioritization than any leadership book ever could, and those lessons come back to how I build and support teams every day.
